About Satellite Web Design

My love for coding started around 12, developing platform computer games to play in the basement with my brother. Simple games led to editing Java code on Runescape private servers to create our own custom worlds. Something about coding really pulled me in. My mom spent her career designing websites which is how I got my start developing. She showed me the basics of HTML & CSS, I taught myself some Javascript, and off I went. Owning a business was always in the back of my mind, so finally I thought, why not combine the two?

My goal with Satellite Web Design is to help small business owners have a better web presence without spending hours of time messing around with page builders or AI. All without breaking the bank. I create simple, effective websites that load in under 1 second, help you rank higher, and will ultimately help you grow your business. This is done by hand writing all of our code with ZERO bloat to slow things down. Our websites score perfect marks on Google Page Insights and are impossible to hack. Did you know that if your website takes more than 3 seconds to load, you could lose up to 50% of your traffic?
